# CarnotEngine — Investor One-Pager

**Problem without CarnotEngine**  
- You cannot *see or prove* what cryptography you run in production (network is blind; static tools guess).
- You can’t quantify **Future Value at Risk (FVaR)** — which valuable data is exposed by weak crypto.
- Audits are slow and subjective; evidence is screenshots and exports that can be manipulated.

**What CarnotEngine does**  
- Observes **runtime cryptography** at the endpoint (eBPF/ETW/JFR) with near‑zero overhead.
- Proves **what was negotiated** (cipher/KEM group) and **where** (workload, host).
- Issues **receipt‑backed attestations** (Merkle + signatures) and a **transparency log** auditors verify.
- Outputs a **Cryptographic Context Map (CCM)** and **FVaR** to prioritize fixes by dollar‑impact.

**Why now**  
- PQC standards are final; mandates (OMB M‑23‑02, CNSA 2.0) require inventory & migration.
- Modern protocols (TLS 1.3, QUIC, ECH) blind network inspection → endpoint truth is mandatory.

**Business model**  
- Fixed‑price **Discovery‑Only Assessment** to land the account.
- Subscription for **continuous monitoring, policy, and verifiable attestations**.
