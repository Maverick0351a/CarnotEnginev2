# Brand Narrative — Reversing Digital Entropy

Organizations drown in encrypted, opaque data flows. Network is blind (TLS 1.3, QUIC, ECH), code scanners guess,
and audits are manual and brittle. **CarnotEngine** observes cryptography **at the endpoint**, correlates facts with
workload context and data lineage seeds, and binds everything with **cryptographic receipts** (Signet).
We transform **unknowns** into **verifiable understanding** and quantify **Future Value at Risk (FVaR)**.

**Plain‑English**: we show *what actually happened* cryptographically, *where*, *by whom*, and we produce evidence
an auditor can verify independently.
